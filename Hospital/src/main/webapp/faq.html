Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Category Question Answer
General What is the purpose of the Hospital Management System? The system is designed to streamline operations such as patient registration, doctor scheduling, billing, and medical record management, ensuring better efficiency and accuracy.
General Who can access the system? The system is accessible by administrators, doctors, nurses, and other authorized personnel, each with role-based access.
Patient How do patients book appointments? Patients can book appointments through the online portal or by contacting the hospital reception directly.
Patient Can I view my medical records online? Yes, registered patients can log in to their accounts to view their medical history, lab results, and prescriptions.
Technical What happens if I forget my password? You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and following the instructions.
Technical Is my data secure? Yes, the system employs robust security measures, including data encryption and secure access protocols, to protect your information.
Support How do I contact support? You can reach our support team via the "Contact Us" page or by calling the hospital helpline.
